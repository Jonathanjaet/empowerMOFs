Motif

The source of inspiration of this first topical conference within this field arises from the main goal of promoting women researching within the field of MOFs and similar materials, striving to be a turning point in the history of this field. For this reason, all talks will be presented by women, thus promoting the broad visibility of their work. The audience will be of mixed gender, encouraging diversity in an inclusive forum for scientific discussions. Conference activities will include networking, scientific workshops and round-tables, supporting active communication. Additionally, the conference will host activities related to spreading awareness regarding gender equity and equality, with the aim of working toward a more diverse environment within the STEM fields.

The ambition of the conference is to unify women in science: undergraduate students, postgraduate students, PhD students, postdoctoral researchers and established researchers, giving all participants the tools to establish an engaging and close-knit community, promoting mentoring, which will empower young women to gain role models within their specific research field.

Although the term “women” is used in the context of this conference, we invite and welcome all attendees who identify as women, transgender, agender, genderqueer, non-binary, and Two-Spirit.
