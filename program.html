Program

A Young Investigator Symposium in Mexico City will be focused on young women within the area of MOFs, COFs and similar materials will be the first “ice-breaker scenario”, with the goal to promote interaction, collaboration, networking, as well as mentoring, establishing a unified community of women in the area. The second part of the conference has been designed to engage participants in workshops and poster sessions in an informal and welcoming atmosphere, near the city of Merida, on the Yucatan peninsula of Mexico.

With the aim of reaching a larger audience and participation, both nationally within Mexico and internationally, the conference is held at two locations in Mexico: Mexico City, the epicenter for the MOF community in Mexico and, Merida, a hub for Mexican culture, history, and nature. The two parts of the conference will have distinct agendas within the scope of the conference and options for participants to participate in both or one of the two sections.
